# Code of Conduct — FitStream

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards
Positive: empathy, respect, constructive feedback, accountability.
Unacceptable: harassment, trolling, insults, doxxing.

## Enforcement
Report to **conduct@fitstream.dev**. All complaints reviewed promptly.
